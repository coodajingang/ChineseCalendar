Calendar Quiz

First draft: August, 2021, Last Update: Jan 7, 2022


The following quiz test your knowledge of Chinese and Western calendar. Several questions contain randomized content.

Unless otherwise stated, "Chinese calendar" here refers to the modern version of the Chinese calendar. That's the calendar calculated based on the rules stated in the official document "Calculation and promulgation of the Chinese calendar" (GB/T 33661-2017).
